About

'From The Jump' was founded by Anshul Adlakha, an Artist Manager with years of experience in music and a professional background in Social Media Marketing. "Creating communities that scale themselves" is how we define our vision and hence, our artist management work broadly revolves around community building via the means of content creation. The goal is to always tap into the unique brand pillars of the artists that help them stand out and establish a genuine connection with the audience.
